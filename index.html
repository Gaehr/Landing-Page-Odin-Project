The gallery of crusty critters

Crabs are crusteacans and amazing animals, found all over the globe. Some live in saltwater, some in freshwater or even on land. They come in all kind of forms and shapes. You can watch them, play with them, or even cook and eat them. Even nature loves them, leading other crusteacans on the evolutionary path to become a crab through carcinization.
